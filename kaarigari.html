The Art of Kaarigari

Preserving ancestral master techniques.

Every piece born in our workshops owes its soul to hereditary master Kaarigars. Their families have lived and breathed the complex geometry of fine stone setting and enameled artistry for generations.

Ancestral Kundan Settings

Unlike modern adhesive castings, our artisans manually compress highly refined gold foil layer by layer around each individual stone. This traditional technique creates a seamless, custom seal that completely encases the diamond or gem, giving it an unmistakable heritage glow.

The Fire of Meenakari

Our signature pastel enamel stories are achieved through intricate, microscopic gravity painting. Mineral powders are carefully engraved directly into channels within the gold frame and subsequently fired in high-heat kilns, creating a vitreous luster designed to withstand lifetimes of wear.

Custodians of the Art

We do not merely employ craftsmen; we partner with them. By providing fair, ethical wages, preserving a safe studio ecosystem, and celebrating their names, we ensure that the invaluable human heritage of Indian jewelry making is sustained. Every curve, fracture, and solder on a Fire & Ice design is a direct expression of a protected heirloom lineage.

Conscious Provenance

True value runs deeper than the surface shine. We mandate strict ethical accountability across our entire supply matrix—working exclusively with verified, conflict-free stone providers. Furthermore, our workshops prioritize the integration of responsibly reclaimed gold and repurposed raw metals, ensuring that our commitment to capturing heritage respects the balance of tomorrow.
