Atelier Preservation Dos

  • Store Independently: Keep your kundan and polki pieces wrapped in soft clean linen or velvet pouches within an airtight container to avoid scratching your gold finishes.
  • Apply Cosmetics First: Ensure all luxury fragrances, fine hairsprays, and alcohol-based cosmetics are absorbed completely before dressing in your fine jewelry.
  • Verify Links Regularly: Inspect your customizable modular safety hinges and safety locks carefully before wearing pieces to formal gatherings.
  • Wipe Gentry: After each wear, use an ultra-soft micro-abrasive cloth to delicately clean accumulated skin oils from metal frames before locking them away.

Preservation Don'ts

  • Avoid Chemical Immersion: Never expose delicate traditional meenakari enamels or kundan foil settings to liquid jewel cleaning solutions or ultrasonic tanks.
  • Protect From Moisture: Keep traditional jadau work completely clear of tap water, steam rooms, pools, and humid storage spaces to avoid darkening the foil seals.
  • Eliminate Abrasive Impact: Avoid wearing substantial ceremonial pieces during active sports or cleanups to preserve the technical alignments of your settings.
  • Avoid Direct Heat: Prevent placing gemstones or soft natural pearl strands close to direct high heaters or continuous harsh sunlight to avoid material cracking.
