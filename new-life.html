The Remodeling Lifecycle

We believe that family heritage shouldn't be locked away out of sight. Our signature heirloom reworking pipeline begins with a thorough material assessment, utilizing non-destructive testing methodology to precisely verify gold purities and confirm stone conditions.

Following our custom personal consultation, our master artisans unmount every diamond, pearl, or gemstone with deep physical sensitivity. We preserve these irreplaceable, original architectural details completely, extracting them safely from their old, heavy structures.

Using these original design elements as our creative anchor, we proceed to build precise scale mockups, allowing you to visualize your jewelry's technical balance. The raw metals are then carefully refined and forged into breathable gold frameworks—transforming your ancestral legacy into a striking statement designed to be proudly worn and lived in today.
